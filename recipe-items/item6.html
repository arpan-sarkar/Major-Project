Chilli Chicken

Chili Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with tender chicken pieces, bell peppers, and onions, all tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce for flavor.

Ready in:

30 min - 45 min

Ingredients:

19

Ingredients

For the Chilli Chicken:

  • Boneless chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces): 500g
  • Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Vinegar (preferably white or rice vinegar): 1 tbsp
  • Cornstarch (for marination): 1 tbsp
  • Black pepper: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil (for frying): 3-4 tbsp

For the Sauce:

  • Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Chili sauce (adjust to taste): 1 tbsp
  • Tomato ketchup: 1 tbsp
  • Sugar (optional, to balance flavors): 1 tsp
  • Black pepper: 1/2 tsp
  • Water: 1/4 cup

For Stir-Frying:

  • Onion (sliced): 1 large
  • Bell peppers (sliced, any color): 1-2
  • Green chilies (sliced, adjust to taste): 2-3
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tbsp
  • Spring onions (for garnish): 1-2
  • Sesame oil (optional, for flavor): 1 tsp

Making process

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, soy sauce, vinegar, cornstarch, black pepper, and salt. Mix well until the chicken is coated.
  2. Let it marinate for 15-30 minutes. If you're short on time, you can skip this step.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, sugar, black pepper, and water. Set aside.

Step 3: Fry the Chicken:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated chicken pieces in batches (don't overcrowd the pan).
  2. Fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the fried chicken and set aside on a paper towel to absorb excess oil.

Step 4: Stir-Fry Vegetables:

  1. In the same pan, add a bit more oil if needed. Add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.

Step 5: AAdd Ginger-Garlic Paste:

  1. Add the ginger-garlic paste and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 6: ombine Chicken and Sauce:

  1. Add the fried chicken back into the pan with the vegetables.
  2. Pour in the prepared sauce and stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables evenly. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.

Step 7: Finish and Serve:

  1. Drizzle with sesame oil (if using) and garnish with chopped spring onions.
  2. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.
