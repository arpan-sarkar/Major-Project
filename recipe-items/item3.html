Chicken Grilled

Grilled Chicken: marinated in a blend of spices and herbs, then perfectly charred on the grill, creating juicy, tender meat with a delicious smoky flavor.

Ready in:

1 hrs - 1.5 hrs

Ingredients:

10

Ingredients

For the Chicken Grilled:

  • Chicken breasts (or thighs, skin-on or skinless, as preferred): 4
  • Olive oil: 3 tbsp
  • Lemon juice (freshly squeezed): 3 tbsp
  • Garlic (minced): 1 tbsp
  • Paprika: 1 tsp
  • Dried oregano: 1 tsp
  • Dried thyme: 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1/2 tsp
  • Black pepper: 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder (optional, for a bit of heat): 1/2 tsp

Making process

Step 1: Prepare the Marinade:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 2: Marinate the Chicken:

  1. Place the chicken breasts in a large ziplock bag or shallow dish.
  2. Pour the marinade over the chicken, ensuring each piece is well coated.
  3. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes (or up to 2 hours for deeper flavor).

Step 3: Preheat the Grill:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 400째F or 200째C).
  2. Lightly oil the grill grates to prevent sticking.

Step 4: Grill the Chicken:

  1. Place the marinated chicken on the grill.
  2. Grill each side for about 5-7 minutes, depending on thickness, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (75째C) and the chicken has nice grill marks.
  3. Avoid flipping too often to allow for good charring and even cooking.

Step 5: Rest and Serve:

  1. Let the grilled chicken rest for a few minutes after cooking to retain juices.
  2. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired, and serve with sides like salad, rice, or grilled veggies.
