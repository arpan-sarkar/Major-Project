Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian dish featuring soft paneer cubes in a creamy, spiced spinach gravy. It's nutritious, flavorful, and perfect with naan or rice.

Ready in:

30 min - 40 min

Ingredients:

17

Ingredients

For the Palak Paneer

  • Paneer (cubed): 250g
  • Fresh spinach (palak): 250g
  • Oil or ghee: 2 tbsp
  • Onion (finely chopped): 1 medium
  • Tomatoes (pureed): 2
  • Garlic (minced): 2-3 cloves
  • Ginger (grated): 1 inch
  • Green chilies (adjust to taste): 1-2
  • Cumin seeds: 1/2 tsp
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala: 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder (optional, adjust to taste): 1/2 tsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Cream or milk (for a creamier texture): 2 tbsp cream or 1/4 cup milk
  • Lemon juice (optional, for garnish)

Making process

Step 1: Blanch the Spinach:

  1. Rinse the spinach leaves thoroughly and bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the spinach leaves and cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  3. Drain the spinach and immediately transfer it to a bowl of ice water to retain its bright green color.

Step 2: Make the Spinach Puree:

  1. Drain the spinach from the ice water and blend it with green chilies to a smooth puree. Set aside.

Step 3: Sauté the Paneer (Optional):

  1. If you prefer a slightly crisp paneer, lightly fry the paneer cubes in a bit of oil until golden brown. This step is optional.

Step 4: Cook the Masala Base:

  1. In a large pan, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the ginger and garlic, and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 5: Add Tomato Puree and Spices:

  1. Add the tomato puree to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil begins to separate.
  2. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 6: Add Spinach Puree and Simmer:

  1. Pour the spinach puree into the pan, stir to combine, and let it cook for about 5 minutes on low heat.
  2. Stir in garam masala and adjust salt if needed.

Step 7: Add Paneer and Cream:

  1. Add the paneer cubes to the spinach gravy and gently stir to coat.
  2. Pour in cream or milk for added creaminess. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 8: Serve:

  1. Turn off the heat and add a squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
  2. Garnish with a drizzle of fresh cream and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
