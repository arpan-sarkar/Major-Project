Fruit Punch

Fruit Punch is a vibrant, sweet and tangy mocktail made with mixed fruit juices, soda, and chopped fruits—perfect for parties, celebrations, or summer refreshment.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

7

Ingredients

For the Fruit Punch:

  • Orange juice: 1/2 cup
  • Pineapple juice: 1/2 cup
  • Apple juice or mixed fruit juice: 1/2 cup
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Chopped fruits (apple, orange, pineapple): 1/2 cup
  • Chilled soda or lemon drink: 1/2 cup
  • Ice cubes: as needed

Making process

Step 1: Mix the Juices:

  1. In a large bowl or jug, combine orange, pineapple, apple juices, and lemon juice.

Step 2: Add Fruits and Soda:

  1. Add chopped fruits and gently stir.
  2. Just before serving, pour chilled soda and mix lightly.

Step 3: Serve:

  1. Pour into serving glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Garnish with fruit slices or mint leaves and serve immediately.
