Mango Fizz

Mango Fizz is a refreshing summer drink made with ripe mango pulp, soda, lemon juice, and mint—delivering a sweet, tangy, and fizzy tropical experience.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

6

Ingredients

For the Mango Fizz:

  • Ripe mango pulp: 1/2 cup (fresh or canned)
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Sugar or honey: 1–2 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Chilled soda water: 3/4 cup
  • Mint leaves: a few (for garnish)
  • Ice cubes: as needed

Making process

Step 1: Blend the Base:

  1. In a blender, combine mango pulp, lemon juice, and sugar/honey.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick.

Step 2: Assemble the Drink:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add the mango mixture till halfway.
  3. Top with chilled soda water and stir gently.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with mint leaves or a mango slice.
  2. Serve immediately for a fizzy, refreshing delight.
