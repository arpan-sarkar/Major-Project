Pineapple Ginger Cooler

Pineapple Ginger Cooler is a refreshing summer drink made with tangy pineapple juice, zesty ginger, lemon, and mint — perfect for cooling down and boosting digestion.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

6

Ingredients

For the Cooler:

  • Fresh pineapple juice: 1.5 cups
  • Ginger juice (freshly grated and strained): 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Mint leaves: a handful
  • Honey or sugar syrup: 1 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes: as needed

Making process

Step 1: Prepare the Base:

  1. In a blender, add pineapple juice, ginger juice, lemon juice, and sweetener.
  2. Add a few mint leaves and blend briefly to infuse flavor.

Step 2: Serve:

  1. Pour the cooler into glasses filled with ice.
  2. Garnish with mint leaves or a pineapple wedge and serve chilled.
