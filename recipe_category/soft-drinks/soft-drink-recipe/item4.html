Mint Mojito

Mint Mojito is a refreshing summer drink made with muddled mint leaves, lemon juice, sugar, and soda. Cool, fizzy, and perfect for hot days.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

6

Ingredients

For the Mojito:

  • Fresh mint leaves: 10–12
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Sugar (or sugar syrup): 1–2 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Salt: a pinch (optional)
  • Soda water or sparkling water: 1 cup
  • Ice cubes: as needed

Making process

Step 1: Muddle the Mint:

  1. In a glass, add mint leaves, sugar, and lemon juice.
  2. Muddle gently with a muddler or back of a spoon to release mint oils.

Step 2: Add Ice and Soda:

  1. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in soda water and stir gently to combine.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with mint sprig and a lemon slice.
  2. Serve immediately while fizzy and chilled.
