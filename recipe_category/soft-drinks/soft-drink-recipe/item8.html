Lemonade

Lemonade is a classic, refreshing drink made with lemon juice, sugar, and water. It’s tangy, sweet, and perfect for staying cool during hot summer days.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

4

Ingredients

For the Lemonade:

  • Lemon juice (fresh): 3 tbsp
  • Sugar: 2 tbsp (adjust to taste)
  • Cold water: 1.5 cups
  • Ice cubes: as needed

Making process

Step 1: Dissolve the Sugar:

  1. In a jug or large glass, add sugar and lemon juice.
  2. Stir until sugar is fully dissolved.

Step 2: Add Water and Ice:

  1. Pour in cold water and mix well.
  2. Add ice cubes to chill the lemonade.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with a lemon slice or mint leaves if desired.
  2. Serve immediately.
