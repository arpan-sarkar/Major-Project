Apple Cider Mocktail

Apple Cider Mocktail is a crisp, sparkling drink combining apple juice, spices, and soda. A perfect non-alcoholic beverage with festive flavor, served chilled with ice.

Ready in:

10 min

Ingredients:

7

Ingredients

For the Mocktail:

  • Apple juice or cider: 1 cup (unsweetened preferred)
  • Cinnamon powder: 1/4 tsp
  • Clove powder (optional): a pinch
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Sparkling water or club soda: 1/2 cup
  • Ice cubes: as needed
  • Apple slices & mint leaves: for garnish

Making process

Step 1: Mix the Base:

  1. In a shaker or large glass, mix apple juice, cinnamon, clove, and lemon juice.

Step 2: Add Sparkle:

  1. Add ice cubes and top with sparkling water. Stir gently.

Step 3: Garnish and Serve:

  1. Garnish with fresh apple slices and mint leaves.
  2. Serve chilled for a refreshing mocktail experience.
